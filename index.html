About Us Artquisite

The Connection between our Art and Your Heart.

We are Manogna & Sowmya, the creators behind Artquisite. We work mainly on traditional art. However, we experiment on various forms and mediums. Also, painting landscapes and portraits have been our passion for over a decade now and nature has always been our true inspiration.
The last two years have been a lot to take in, and during this time, we created the most we have ever done in the last few years, and when we look back at some of the artworks we've created over the years, we realize that it's been quite a rewarding experience. A journey full of learning and growing together. Every painting is a discovery; each stroke we make, each surface we shade brings out a whole new picture, sometimes unpredictable, sometimes good, but most of the time terrible. Sometimes it gets exhausting to spend hours and hours creating or shooting while everything is hauling around, or simply not being consistent sucks, but it also gives us time to process and get better. Sometimes we tend to compare with other artists, but along the way, it's just you enjoying what you do no matter how perfect or imperfect that is; you just have to keep creating!
