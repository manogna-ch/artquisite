About Us Artquisite

Emotion is intrinsic to creativity, an exploration of interior spaces.

...I view interiority as a space of time travel, a layering of time, between fragmented thoughts â€“ a process of discovering yourself through art.
We are Hyderabad-based artists working mainly on traditional art. However, we experiment on various forms and mediums. Also, painting landscapes and portraits has been our passion for over a decade now. Nature has always been our true inspiration.

We have always been drawn to painting for as long as we can remember, but gradually, it deteriorated as we started doing other things in life. However, for some reason, I always wanted to create a platform to showcase my art, and after a few attempts of deleting and starting over, I decided to go ahead and do it one final time, but now I am glad that I made this decision. When I started this page back in 2016, I let it stay anonymous for over a year. However, slowly when I started encountering people in the art community, I felt a little confident. Two years down the line, we became a team of two, and since then, we have complemented each other and pushed ourselves to keep practising and getting better at one thing at a time. Who knew that 5 years later, we would be here to take another leap on a journey of growth. These last few years were about trying new mediums, and moving forward; it will be more about getting control over the medium.

Last two years have been a lot to take in, and during this time, we created the most we have ever done in the last 6 years, and that made it so rewarding, and when we look back at some of the artworks we've created over the years, it's been quite a journey. A journey full of learning and growing together. Every painting is a discovery; each stroke we make, each surface we shade brings out a whole new picture, sometimes unpredictable, sometimes good, but most of the time terrible. Sometimes it gets exhausting to spend hours and hours creating or shooting while everything is hauling around, or simply not letting the paint flow and not being consistent sucks. However, in times like these, Instagram has been a source of inspiration. Sometimes we tend to compare with other artists, but along the way, it's just you enjoying what you do no matter how perfect or imperfect that is; just keep creating!

Creating art has not been the only thing, but connecting with artists worldwide has also made us discover ourselves and find our identity. We are grateful that we could meet amazing artists, and the level of inspiration that goes around with like-minded people is just crazy.

Here's a special mention to all our friends and family. You give us purpose, a reason to get out of bed each morning and do our best work. We could not do it without your endless support, inspiration, and if words of gratitude were balloons, we would be soaring above the clouds.
